About Me

I am a passionate bioinformatics researcher with expertise in computational genomics, molecular biology, and programming. My focus lies in integrating computational methods with biological insights to drive innovations in genomics and personalized medicine.

With a background in both wet lab and dry lab techniques, I aim to bridge the gap between experimental biology and computational analysis, enabling precise biomarker discovery and disease modeling.

My research interests include cancer genomics, protein structural analysis, single-cell transcriptomics, and the application of AI in bioinformatics.
